8.2  Focal priorities

“The best way to predict the future is to invent it.”
                         —Alan Kay

One current focus of the Mythryl project proper is improving stability and usability by fixing known bugs and improving documentation.

Another current focus is building and improving development-critical infrastructure, such as an IDE and debugger, good interfaces to major C libraries, and an archive network for sharing, indexing and installing packages written in Mythryl.

Comments and suggestions to: bugs@mythryl.org
